SonoKnee

v1.0 · WebXR

A hands-on VR trainer for the ultrasound-guided intra-articular knee injection, built for family medicine residents. Uses the lateral suprapatellar recess approach with an in-plane needle.

The task

1
Hold the transducer (left controller) transversely, just above the patella, to find the suprapatellar recess.
2
Watch the live sono screen — the dark recess sits between the quadriceps and prefemoral fat pads.
3
Advance the needle (right controller) lateral → medial, in-plane, keeping the tip on screen.
4
Pull the trigger to inject once the tip is seated in the recess. Avoid the quadriceps tendon.

Desktop preview: drag to orbit · ←→ move probe · Space advance needle · Enter inject.

Why this approach

Ultrasound guidance raises intra-articular knee injection accuracy to ~95% vs ~82% for landmark technique (13-study systematic review, Am J Phys Med Rehabil, 2025).1 The lateral suprapatellar recess, in-plane, lateral-to-medial route reached the joint on the first pass in 94% of cases and avoids the quadriceps tendon.2,3
